Aviso de Privacidad — App para Meseras

Última actualización: 12/06/2026

Esta app no recopila, vende ni comparte ningún dato personal.

Privacy Notice — App for Waitresses

Last updated: 06/12/2026

This app does not collect, sell, or share any personal data.

📱 Your data stays on your device

All your shift records, schedules, earnings, and notes are stored only on your phone. They are never sent to any server or shared with anyone.

📡 100% offline capable

The app works completely without an internet connection. Your financial information never leaves your device.

☁️ Optional developer connection

The only network request the app makes is to fetch donation contact info (email, Facebook, PayPal, Mercado Pago) from a public file on GitHub. This runs in the background, contains no user data, and is entirely optional.

🚫 No accounts or registration

No account, email, or personal data is required to use this app.

🔒 Local and secure PIN

Your PIN is never stored as plain text. It is saved as a cryptographic hash that cannot be reversed.

📧 Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact smy42325@gmail.com.
